## Context

The forces at play: constraints, prior art, what triggered the decision. One or two paragraphs is plenty. State the problem in terms a future reader can recognise without rereading the rest of the repo.

## Decision

What we're doing, in one or two sentences. Active voice.

## Consequences

What becomes easier, what becomes harder, what we explicitly accept. List both the wins and the costs — an ADR with no costs is hiding something.
